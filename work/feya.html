Feya Candle Co.

Redesigned packaging for Feya’s 15 original candle scents, as well as 5 additional as a part of “the wanderlust collection”. Additional work with Feya includes design for Feya Soaps, as well as their catalog. Each product maintains a consistent template yet contains unique graphic elements and colors to each.

Feya Candle Co. partners with organizations working to improve communities around the world. They’ve worked with organizations like: Jacob’s Well – Haiti, Haiti Chidren’s Hope, and a community organization in Chontales, Nicaragua. When Feya gives food, they fund the purchasing of meals in the villages they serve – boosting the local economy, and help in any way they can to serve the food to those who need it most.
